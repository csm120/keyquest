Press Insert Down Arrow to say all from the current position.
Press Insert Up Arrow to read the current line.
Press Insert F12 to hear the time or date.
Press Insert T to hear the window title.
Press Insert Tab to hear progress bar status in some dialog boxes.
Press Insert J to open the JAWS window.
Press Insert V to open Quick Settings.
Press Insert F1 to open context-sensitive help.
Press Insert 1 to turn keyboard help on or off.
Press Insert 3 on the number row to pass the next key through to the application.
Press Insert Escape to refresh the screen.
Press Control to stop speech.
Press Insert B to read the current window from top to bottom.
Press Insert 5 on the NumPad to read the current word.
On web pages, press Insert F6 to list headings.
On web pages, press Insert F7 to list links.
On web pages, press Insert F3 to open the list of page elements.
On web pages, press Insert F5 to list form fields.
On web pages, press H to jump to the next heading.

On web pages, press 1 through 6 to jump to a heading level.

On web pages, press Shift K to jump to the previous link.
